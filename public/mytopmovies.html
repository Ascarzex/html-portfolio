Movies Reviews According to Arturo

Last 3 Movies that I've seen and enjoyed on a cinema plus who I went with hehe

Wicked

I was excited to see this movie with my sister. It did not dissappoint at all. I really enjoyed it from start to finish, almost didn't blink!

Joker 2

For this movie I went with my friend Karen. We were gladly surprised by this movie being a musical. We loved every bit of it. Even sang along some of the songs haha.

Terrifier 3

I went with Alfredo, a guy that I met recently. Most of the time, we would squint our eyes from disgust or shock. It was a movie full of gore and absolutely bizarre scenes. However, we were looking forward to all of that :D
